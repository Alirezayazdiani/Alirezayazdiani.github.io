I am a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Engineering at Cornell University, advised by [Prof. Oliver Gao](https://www.cee.cornell.edu/faculty-directory/h-oliver-gao). My research sits at the intersection of **transportation data science**, **machine learning**, and **optimization** for public-sector decision-making.

I develop Python-based analytical pipelines, predictive and statistical models, and MILP/MINLP optimization frameworks for large-scale mobility, logistics, and environmental planning problems. My work focuses on integrating complex transportation datasets into decision-support tools for **policy evaluation**, **system performance analysis**, and **sustainable infrastructure planning**.

Before Cornell, I received my M.Sc. in Transportation Engineering from Sharif University of Technology (ranked 1st among all M.Sc. students) and my B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of Tehran.
